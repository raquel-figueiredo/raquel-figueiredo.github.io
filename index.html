ABOUT ME

#WomanInTech

Welcome to my website! I'm Raquel Figueiredo, I have 6+ years of experience in technology and I have been in the job market since 2012. Formed in technical Web Design by Etec Vasco Antonio Venchiarutti - ETECVAV; Specialist in the Game Design and Development by School of Art, Game and Animation - SAGA and attending the last year of Bachelor of Computer Science University Center of FACCAMP. In the last years I've been working with Game Design, 2D/3D Modeling and Animation, Front-end Development, and User Experience/UX Design. I worked in several companies with work contracts and freelance jobs; I know how to work as a team and and I'm adept at Agile and Scrum methodologies. I'm part of Prosper Games Studio, indie games and animation studio. I have already had the opportunity of working with large multinational clients from Latin America and some customers in North America, such as: Itaú Unibanco, Age of Learning, Mapfre Seguradora, Zurich Seguros, XP Investimentos, BrasilPrev, Bradesco Seguros, UOL, Bematech by Totvs e Microsoft, running varied projects among Internet Things, Business Intelligence, Design Thinking, Innovation, Interaction Design, Gamification, User Experience and of course, line of code.

On the personal side I have my hobbies: I'm travel lover and lover of extreme sports like parachute jump and go rafting, I like to ride my bike and go to the gym, and there are days I just want to get a good book and spend hours reading. I'm videogame lover so that's why I chose one of the areas to work, one of my dreams is to work only with games and/or animation. I like to watch movies and TV series, I love to be with my cat Teddy and I love to cook.
